A library for multiphysics solution transfer.

The Data Transfer Kit (DTK) is an open-source software library designed to provide parallel services for solution transfer for multiphysics simulations http://www.casl.gov/Infrastructure.shtml. DTK uses a general operator design to provide scalable parallel services for solution transfer between shared volumes and surfaces. DTK is supported and used by the following Department of Energy multiphysics modeling and simulation programs:

  • Consortium for Advanced Simulation of Light Water Reactors (CASL) http://www.casl.gov

  • National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) http://batterysim.org

  • Nuclear Energy Advanced Modeling and Simulation (NEAMS) http://www.ne.anl.gov/NEAMS/

Developers can browse the DTK Doxygen for details on classes and interfaces.
